Understanding Energy Systems

Explore the fundamental concepts of energy sources and energy carriers, with a focus on hydrogen and renewable energy technologies.

Energy Source

Naturally occurring sources of energy, which can be converted to other useful forms of energy.

Energy Carrier

Defined as something with the ability to store or deliver energy from one place to another.

Hydrogen as an Energy Carrier

On earth, hydrogen is an energy carrier, rather than an energy source. It can store tremendous amounts of energy, and only produces water when burnt. However, the means of creating this hydrogen determines whether it is carbon neutral.
